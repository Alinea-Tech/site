About AlineaTech

We partner with agriculture, industrial, and defense organizations that want to improve productivity and team satisfaction but struggle to adopt technology in ways aligned with workforce skills, preferences, and legal/ethical constraints (e.g., AI Act). Our framework optimizes decision‑making in human–machine teams, producing tangible benefits: choosing the right technologies, smoother adoption, higher engagement, and more effective investments. Unlike generic consulting or one‑size‑fits‑all digital tools, we deeply integrate team contributions, context‑specific constraints, and cost‑benefit analysis into technology selection, ensuring operational wins and human alignment, grounded in academic research at leading institutes.

  • Smoother adoption with engaged teams.
  • Investment decisions grounded in cost‑benefit and measurable outcomes.
